How CancerDetect Works

Understanding our AI-powered detection process

The Detection Process

Our 4-step approach to accurate breast cancer detection

1

Data Input

Input 30 medical features from biopsy results including mean values, standard errors, and worst measurements.

2

AI Analysis

Our machine learning model processes the data through multiple neural network layers to identify patterns.

3

Pattern Recognition

The algorithm compares input patterns against thousands of known cases to identify potential malignancies.

4

Results Delivery

Receive immediate analysis with confidence scores and detailed explanations of the findings.

Our Technology Stack

Machine Learning

We use ensemble methods combining Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and Neural Networks for maximum accuracy.

Feature Engineering

Advanced feature selection techniques ensure we focus on the most predictive medical indicators.

Data Security

All data is encrypted and anonymized to protect patient privacy while maintaining analysis quality.
